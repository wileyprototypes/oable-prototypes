This design is for illustrative purposes only.

Design for parent consortia, or normal consortia admin. Using Projekt DEAL as an example.

  • 707 Institutions
  • MPDLS representitives manage requests for some institutions, other individual institution admins manage their own
  • The agreement covers Hybrid and Gold journals
  • The draw down mechanism for the Hybrid and Gold part is a single EUR pot - with the additional caveat that Hybrid requests are charged at a standard rate and should not be visible to consortia admin
